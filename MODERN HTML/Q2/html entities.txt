HTML entities are the reserved characters that are used in the HTML document. They are not present in your standard keyboard. They provide a wide range of characters, allowing you to add icons, geometric shapes, mathematical operators, and so on.\
examples-
< or < for the less than sign (<)
> or > for the greater than sign (>)
& or & for the ampersand sign (&)
" or " for the double quotation mark (")
© or © for the copyright sign (©)
€ or € for the euro sign (€)
® or ® for the registered trademark sign (®)
  or for the non-breaking space
­ or ­ for the soft hyphen
♥ or ♥ for the heart symbol (♥)
